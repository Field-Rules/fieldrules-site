Week Zero

We start by listening.

Week one is not onboarding. It's us interviewing your team in depth about the judgment calls you're encoding into features right now.

We sit with your product leads, your domain experts, your engineers. We read your Slack archives, your design docs, your Jira history. We ask the questions: “What would go wrong if this rule didn't exist? Who carries this logic? What happens in a year if nobody remembers this call?”

By end of week zero, you have a library. By week two, you're enforcing it. By week four, you're reading from it instead of hardcoding.
