The challenge

Why your organization needs this.

AI systems make increasingly critical decisions, yet organizations struggle to understand why those decisions are made. FieldRules solves this by capturing domain expert reasoning as IF/THEN/BECAUSE rules — creating unprecedented auditability, control, and trust.

The problem
Black-box AI systems make high-stakes decisions without explanation. Regulatory pressure, stakeholder concerns, and internal governance gaps demand better solutions.
The opportunity
By encoding domain expert knowledge as versioned rules, you gain complete visibility into AI behavior, improve decision quality, and demonstrate governance to regulators.
How it works

Three steps to auditable AI.

FieldRules transforms domain expertise into auditable AI governance through a simple three-step process.

Capture rules
Work with your domain experts to define IF/THEN/BECAUSE rules that express their reasoning. Each rule captures the logic, the context, and the human judgment behind decisions.
Version & audit
Every rule is versioned, timestamped, and linked to the expert who created it. Track changes, compare versions, and maintain a complete audit trail of governance decisions.
Govern AI
Apply your rules to AI systems in production. FieldRules ensures decisions align with expert reasoning, catching drift and enabling rapid course correction.

Evaluation

Pilot success criteria.

We've defined clear metrics to determine if this pilot is working for your organization.

Governance effectiveness
At least 80% of AI decisions are explainable by captured rules. Clear gaps and edge cases are documented. Your team sees how rules improve decision quality.
Organizational adoption
Domain experts are actively using FieldRules. At least 2 additional teams express interest. Leadership sees value and approves expansion beyond the pilot scope.
Operational efficiency
Audit and governance tasks are faster. Rule versioning provides clear change history. Integrations with your AI systems work smoothly with no major rework required.
Risk & compliance
Regulatory audits are easier with documented rule history. Bias and drift in AI decisions are measurable and addressable. You have concrete evidence of governance.
Your team

Who should pilot with us.

The best pilots include stakeholders from these roles. You don't need all of them, but this mix delivers the fastest results.

Domain experts
Your lending, underwriting, claims, or compliance experts. These are the people who understand why good decisions are made. They'll drive rule capture and validation.
AI / ML engineers
The team building or operating AI systems. They'll handle integration, testing, and measurement. They benefit most from understanding how rules govern AI behavior.
Compliance & risk
Regulatory and risk teams see immediate value in auditability. They'll help define success criteria and validate that rules meet governance requirements.
Leadership sponsor
A director or VP who can clear calendars, ensure resources, and champion the pilot internally. Pilots succeed when they have visible leadership support.
