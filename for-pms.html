THE CORE MECHANISM

The BECAUSE field is what makes your spec possible

The IF tells you what triggers the rule. The THEN tells you what to do. The BECAUSE tells you why this company does it this way — and that's the only part no AI can generate.

IF
Enterprise client requests data export during active contract renegotiation
THEN
Route to account manager first, do not fulfill automatically, flag as retention-sensitive
BECAUSE

WHY NOW

Everything an autonomous agent can do, another agent can replicate. The only thing that can't be replicated is the reasoning your people carry about why your company does things differently.
The more AI automates execution — building products, running outbound, iterating on feedback — the more the defensible layer shifts upstream. To the conditional judgment. The exception-handling logic. The domain expertise that was never written down because it lived in someone's head.
If you're pre-AI
Your operational edge is invisible and unstructured. An autonomous agent could build a commodity version of your product tomorrow. FieldRules makes that edge real before it walks out the door.
If you're deployed-AI
Your AI features are only as defensible as the reasoning layer underneath. System prompts and hardcoded conditionals can be approximated. A BECAUSE-governed rule library is proprietary infrastructure.
If you're an investor
The companies that survive the agent era are the ones whose operational logic is captured, structured, and continuously deepened. FieldRules is the capture mechanism. The longer it runs, the wider the moat.
