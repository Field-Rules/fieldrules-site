The BECAUSE moment

The reasoning Alex needs to spec correctly

This is a real rule about renewal pricing exceptions. The BECAUSE field explains why the 15% threshold exists — reasoning a PM needs to spec correctly and currently doesn't have access to.

💰
Renewal Pricing Exceptions · Confirmed Apr 2026
WHEN
Account requests a renewal discount above the standard 10% tier and the account manager flags it for approval
THEN
Any discount above 15% requires VP-Finance sign-off before the quote is sent. Between 10–15%, CS lead can approve with documented rationale.
BECAUSE
Above 15%, the unit economics flip — the account becomes unprofitable in year 2 once support costs are loaded. We learned this the hard way with three enterprise accounts in Q3 2024 that renewed at 18–22% discounts and all churned within 14 months anyway. The discount didn't save them; it just delayed the conversation we should have had about fit.
