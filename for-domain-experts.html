The Problem

Your expertise is invisible.

You handle 40 tickets a week. In at least 5 of them, you make a judgment call that deviates from the standard playbook — because you know something the playbook doesn't. That deviation is the most valuable thing you do. And it's completely invisible.

It doesn't show up in your performance review. It doesn't get captured in a wiki. It doesn't feed the AI that's supposed to handle cases like yours. It lives in your head until you leave, and then it's gone.

What We Ask

45 seconds. Your words. Your name.

When FieldRules detects a judgment call in your work — a ticket resolution, a Slack thread, a pattern it spotted — it sends you a card. You see the IF/THEN it drafted. You confirm, edit, or dismiss. One tap.

Then it asks you to write the BECAUSE. Not a summary. Not a form. The actual reasoning — in your words, in your voice.
